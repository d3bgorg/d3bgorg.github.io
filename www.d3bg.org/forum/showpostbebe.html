"Hello there!

Looking into this, the problem is actually related to your country, in that we can't offer paypal as a payment method in Bulgaria Which is why you are getting the error message.

This is a little out of hour hands as it is on paypals side as well, but it's currently being looked into to see what we can do!

Sorry about the bad news on this one my friend, and I hope you have a great rest of the day!

Farewell for now! /bow
