Цитат:
In 446 paragon levels I have found the super rare cow rift exactly twice. Upon finding it the second time I decided to record the run. Not even a minute into the first level I find these doors to a Jar of Souls like event. This event, (Udder Madness) which I lovingly call the Jar of Cows spawns an ever increasing number of cows until the 40 second timer runs out. Each cow killed gives 2.61 million xp and 63,810 gold on T4. Towards the end of the encounter The Cow Princess spawns, a purple mob that, unfortunately, dropped nothing.
