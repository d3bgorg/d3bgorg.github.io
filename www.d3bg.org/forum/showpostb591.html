Ето и част от по интересните неща казани на Tavern talk-a(за тези, които не им се гледа цялото видео)
2:00PM: Random trivia questions... seems like they might be running a bit late. Almost 2K viewers.
2:06PM: Looks like we're starting! John Yang, Wyatt Cheng, and Don Vu join Nevalistis.
2:07PM: Quick overview of Seasons and Leaderboards. Nothing too new. Items, XP, etc. roll over after seasons end.
2:11PM: Wyatt Cheng lost a hardcore monk due to getting a glass of water and not pausing the game. lol.
2:12PM: Overview of greater rifts by Wyatt Cheng.
2:14PM: Apparently John Yang plays quite a bit, he's soon to hit P600.
2:15PM: Overview of Leg gems by John Yang. John Yang's favorite gem is Zei's stone of vengeance. Wyatt's is Simplicity's strength. Don Vu's is Mirinae.
2:18PM: Overview of The Vault.
2:20PM: Overview of Dex changes (dodge -> armor). The reason this change occurred wasn't because they thought survivability in 2.0 was a problem; the change was mostly due to greater rift survivability in 2.1. They've had other ideas other than just armor such as 0.5 AR & 0.5 Armor.
2:23PM: It's not true that a single dev works on a single class. For example, people thought Don Vu was the only dev working on wiz and monk, which apparently isn't true. It seems that they rotate responsibilities for each class, almost on a weekly basis. Major balance decisions are made collectively as a team.
2:25PM: Q&A begins.
2:27PM: All 3 of the devs play some amount of hardcore.
2:28PM: Opinions on each class, by John Yang. Most sets should reach 2k-3k weapon damage per second, given that they have their max damage set (e.g. Jade). On Barb: currently most of their spenders/damage don't fit well with a specific set. Their goal isn't to buff the skill itself, but to add a legendary that would, for example, triple the base damage of a given spell (e.g. Hammer). Their approach isn't to buff skills incrementally but to add legendaries that make skills more viable.
2:34PM: Overview of the Roland's crusader set.
2:35PM: Overview of changes to WD's sacrifice. They might add new elements to Sacrifice in the future, but not currently. In general, they're trying to reduce the number of elements each class uses.
2:40PM: Monks: They're pretty happy with where all classes are for 2.1. There are more changes down the line, such as making Raiment stronger and using different playstyles, but this is likely to come after 2.1.
2:41PM: Raiment's set: no concrete changes down the line, but they might add a synergistic legendary later on. They don't want to only buff Raiment's, since they realize the gameplay with Dashing Strike spam is rather annoying.
2:44PM: Leg gems should be viable for everyone, not just people steamrolling T6.
2:45PM: You have higher probability of getting leg gem drops in greater rifts at higher greater rift levels.
2:46PM: John Yang thinks Barbs are going to get a "second life" ability. It would probably replace a passive. Wyatt Cheng disagrees.
2:48PM: Block might get changed to a percentage block instead of a flat damage block. This is a concern because of greater rift scaling making block too weak.
2:51PM: CHC & Crit Damage: This as been talked about a lot and the devs recognize it as a problem. The problem they see is that you need to use two affixes to take advantage of Crit (both CHC and Crit Damage).
2:53PM: Socketing on rings and amulets is a deliberate choice to make people have to choose between CHC & Crit Damage.
2:54PM: They might eliminate Crit Damage in a future season as an experiment, but this is not going to happen in Season 1. They don't have anything concrete right now wrt changes to Crit.
2:55PM: They might add or buff 6-piece set bonuses to the Vanilla sets (e.g. Zuni's, Nat's, IK). These would be build-defining and work completely different than what currently exists (e.g. M6 DH). They are likely not going to nerf M6 significantly as they think it sets the bar for DHes.
2:58PM: Overview of Hellfire amulet. The infernal machine event is unlikely to see too many changes in the future because they would rather focus on new areas, such as the Cesspools or Greed's realm.
3:00PM: They might add or change bounties.
3:01PM: No concrete plans to change clans, communities, social, etc.
3:03PM: Overview of changes with 2Hes. Repeated theme: They don't want to make any change in which, when people to log in, they suddenly feel weaker.
3:05PM: Witch doctor pet survivability is a problem, but should not be solved only by buffing Enforcer gem. They're looking for class-wide changes to pets to buff their survivability to scale with enemy damage. They don't want pets to be too weak, but not invincible either.
3:08PM: Wyatt Cheng's favorite class is... Economics.
3:11PM: It IS possible to transfer characters from UEE on XBOX360 to PS4.
3:13PM: The inability to swap items in GRs will likely stay the way it is. This is because everyone will eventually be forced to micro too much (lol at how this is completely different to the design philosophy in Starcraft...)
3:16PM: Again, possible changes to bounties but nothing concrete to be shared right now.
3:17PM: No planned changes to make it easier to farm for a specific item (e.g. SMK).
3:19PM: All seasons will have some new season-exclusive legendary.
3:21PM: Apparently the smart loot system does take into account your follower.
3:21PM: Experience bar system for leg gems was discussed, but Wyatt has two qualms: he wants it to be RNG to some extent (ugh), but more importantly he wants there to be a significant reward for reaching new Greater rift tiers (e.g. instead of a system where going one more GR level up only gives a small 2-3% bonus to your upgrade probability).
3:24PM: Wyatt on Alkaizer's GR comments: it's very possible that Alkaizer is right in that Leg Gem and GR rewards are too weak. XP and legendaries in GRs may be buffed in the future.
3:27PM: THEIR MAIN DESIGN GOAL IN CAPPING LEG GEMS BASED ON GR LEVELS IS BECAUSE THEY WANT TO MAKE IT VERY REWARDING TO PUSH A HIGHER GR LEVEL. This means that it is unlikely that they are going to change the current system where Leg gems are tied to GRs. Ugh, this is so disappointing.
3:29PM: Seasons will be added to consoles in the future, but not now.
3:30PM: Changes to Wizards: they want to make skills (e.g. magic weapon) more active, like mantras. No concrete plans at the moment. Don Vu mumbling.
3:32PM: Changes to Helltooth / Wall of Zombies: Wall of Zombies might be changed to something like a ring that entraps enemies. They didn't want to just buff the damage to the set.
3:34PM: WHEN IS THE RELEASE DATE FOR 2.1? NO RELEASE DATE YET. THE GOAL IS THE END OF THE MONTH BUT THE REAL ANSWER IS AS SOONTM AS THE PATCH IS READY
3:35PM: AT MIDNIGHT FOR YOUR REGION, THERE WILL BE A 50% BUFF TO GOLDFIND, WHICH STACKS MULTIPLICATIVELY WITH ALL OTHER GOLDFIND BONUSES. (Who cares about gold...?)
3:38PM: Done!
