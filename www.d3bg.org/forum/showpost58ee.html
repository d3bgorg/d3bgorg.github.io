    The redesigned Skull Grasp will be additive with the new Barbarian Set.

    The Chest piece of the new Demon Hunter Set will actually be a Cloak, not a regular chest armor.

    Ancient Items will not look different from "regular" Legendaries on the ground, you can only tell them apart after being identified.

    The devs agree that you're currently doing GR Trials too often and are looking into it.

    Instead of buffing Furnace drop rates, new and more interesting Legendary weapons will be implemented.
