По - долу ще може да видите всичк най-важно и необходимо, също така на банера по-долу ще може да видит всички налични неща до момента в ПТР-а.


*Три нови специфични за даден клас сетове : Wrath of the Wastes, Unhallowed Essence and Delsere's Magnum Opus.

*Подобрени "стари" сетове.

*Много нови легендарни итеми.

*Нови легендарни афикси към стари легендарни, които са нямали.

*Преработени стари легендарни - като Schaeffer's Hammer, Death Watch Mantle и др.

*Нови постижения, Завоевания и нов вид козметични награди.

*&"Дрешник" и ново козметично меню - Крилата вече ще се съхраняват в него.

*Три нови гоблина: Insufferable Miscreant, the Gelatinous Sire, и the Gilded Baron. единствено може да ги срещнете в Adventure mode.

*Обикновените Health Potions - те са премахнати от дропа и са с нов дизайн като легендарна колба, която може да я пускате колкото пъти искате.

*Промени при задаването на бройка за крафт на итеми или джемове. Вече е необходимо да изчакате завършването на 1 бройка, а не на всички.

*Крафтинг материалите и джемовете, които са от един вид в разстояние от 20 ярда ще се взимат автоматично след като сте прибрали някой от тях.


Всичи промени в ПТР-а

Нови сетове

Wrath of the Wastes

(2) Increase the damage of Rend by 500% and its duration to 15 seconds
(4) While Whirlwinding you take 40% reduced damage
(6) Whirlwind gains the effect of the Dust Devils rune and the damage of the Dust Devils is increased to 2500% weapon damage

Unhallowed Essence

(2) Your generators also generate 1 Discipline
(4) Gain 20% damage reduction and 20% increased damage for 4 seconds if no enemy is within 10 yards of you
(6) Your generators and Multishot deal 15% more damage for every point of Discipline you have

Delsere's Magnum Opus

(2) Casting Arcane Orb, Energy Twister, Magic Missile or Shock Pulse reduces the cooldown of your Slow Time by 2 seconds
(4) Enemies affected by your Slow Time take 2000% weapon damage every second
This damage type will be determined by your highest equipped +% Elemental Damage
(6) Enemies affected by your Slow Time take 750% more damage from your Arcane Orb, Energy Twister, Magic Missile and Shock Pulse

Старите преработени сетове

Bastions of Will

(2) Set Bonus
After casting a resource-generating attack or primary skill, gain 50% increased damage for 5 seconds
Your first hit with each cast of a resource-spending attack, grants 50% increased damage for 5 seconds

Bul-Kathos's Oath

(2) Set Bonus
Increases Fury generation per second by 10
During Whirlwind gain 30% increased Attack Speed and Movement Speed

Roland's Legacy

(2) Every use of Shield Bash and Sweep Attack reduces the cooldown of your Laws and Defensive Skills by 1 second
(4) Damage bonus increased from 100% to 500%. Damage bonus is now multiplicative
(6) Attack Speed bonus increased from 20% to 30% per stack

Immortal King's Call

(4) Reduce the cooldown of Wrath of the Berserker and Call of the Ancients by 3 seconds for every 10 Fury you spend with an attack
(6) [new] While both Wrath of the Berserker and Call of the Ancients are active, you deal 100% increased damage
Now has an additional set piece: Legs (Immortal King's Stature)

Immortal King's Boulder Breaker - Now rolls 45-60% Call of the Ancients damage as a fifth primary affix

Natalya's Vengeance

(2) The cooldown of Rain of Vengeance is reduced by 2 seconds every time you cast a primary skill or from the first hit of each cast of a resource-spending attack
(4) Rain of Vengeance deals 100% increased damage
(6) [new] - After casting Rain of Vengenace, deal 400% increased damage and take 30% reduced damage for 5 seconds
Now has additional set pieces: Gloves (Natalya's Touch) and Legs (Natalya's Leggings)

Natalya's Slayer - Now rolls +20-25% Rain of Vengeance damage as a fifth primary affix

Inna's Mantra

(2) Increase the passive effect of your Mystic Ally and the base passive effect of your Mantra by 100%
(6) [new] - Mystic Ally casts Cyclone Strike, Exploding Palm, Lashing Tail Kick, Seven-Sided Strike, and Wave of Light when you do
Now has additional set pieces: Gloves (Inna's Hold) and Boots (Inna's Sandals)
Inna's Reach - Now rolls +20-25% Mystic Ally damage as a fifth primary affix

Raiment of a Thousand Storms

(2) Your Spirit Generators have 25% increased attack speed and 300% increased damage
(4) Dashing Strike now spends 75 Spirit, but refunds a Charge when it does
(6) Your Spirit Generators increase the weapon damage of Dashing Strike to 12500% for 6 seconds
Quality of Life: Dashing Stike now lights up on your action bar when the increased weapon damage is active

Raiment of the Jade Harvester

(2) [new] When Haunt lands on an enemy already affected by Haunt, it instantly deals 10 seconds worth of Haunt damage
(4) [new] - Soul Harvest gains the effect of every rune
(6) Amount of DoT time detonated increased from 30 to 40 seconds

Zunimassa's Haunt

(2) Your Fetish Army lasts until they die
(4) You and your pets take 2% reduced damage for every Fetish you have alive
(6) [new] - Enemies hit by your Mana spenders take 275% more damage from your pets for 4 seconds
Now has additional set pieces: Gloves (Zunimassa's Finger Wraps) and Legs (Zunimassa's Cloth)
Zunimassa's String of Skulls - Now rolls +20-25% Fetish Army damage as a fifth primary affix

Firebird's Finery

(2) When you die, a meteor falls from the sky and revives you. This effect has a 300 second cooldown
The meteor that falls from the sky is now a Molten Impact Meteor
(4) Your Fire Spells cause up to 3 enemies to explode, dealing 400% weapon damage as Fire to enemies within 10 yards
Bug Fixes - Fixed an issue that prevented the cooldown debuff from being removed on death

Tal Rasha's Elements

(2) Damaging enemies with Arcane, Cold, Fire or Lightning will cause a Meteor of the same damage type to fall from the sky. There is an 8 second cooldown for each damage type
Now uses the Molten Impact rune when triggered by Fire damage
Can no longer be triggered by hitting a destructible object
(4) Attacks increase your resistance to that element by 100% for 6 seconds
(6) Attacks increase your damage by 150% for 6 seconds. Arcane, Cold, Fire, and Lightning attacks each add one stack (maximum of 4). Adding a stack refreshes the duration
Now has additional set pieces: Gloves (Tal Rasha's Grasp) and Legs (Tal Rasha's Stride)

Tal Rasha's Unwavering Glare - Now rolls +20-25% Meteor damage as a fifth primary affix
Bug Fixes - Fixed an issue that prevented Magic Weapon: Electrify from triggering this set bonus

Captain Crimson's Trimmings

(2) Life per Second increased from 2000 to 6000

Guardian's Jeopardy

(2) Life per Second increased from 2000 to 8000

Добавени легендарни афикси на стари легендарни, които са нямали :

Obsidian Ring of the Zodiac [Seasonal]

Your first hit with each cast of a resource-spending attack reduces the remaining cooldown of one of your skills by 1 second

Broken Promises

After five consecutive non-critical hits, your chance to critically hit is increased by 100% for 3 seconds

Nagelring [Seasonal]

Summons a Fallen Lunatic to your side every 10-12 seconds

Oculus Ring

Chance to create an area of focused power on killing a monster. Damage is increased by 35-40% while standing in the area

Gavel of Judgment

Now rolls with +30-40% Hammer of the Ancients damage as a fifth primary affix
Hammer of the Ancients returns 15-20 Fury if it hits 3 or less enemies

Skull Grasp

Increases the damage of Whirlwind by 300-400% weapon damage

Denial

Each enemy hit by your Sweep Attack increases the damage of your next Sweep Attack by 30-40%, stacking up to 5 times

Ivory Tower

Blocks release forward a Fires of Heaven

Dead Man's Legacy [Seasonal]

Multishot hits enemies who are below 50-60% health twice

Triumvirate

Your Signature Spells increase the damage of Arcane Orb by 75-100% for 6 seconds, stacking up to 3 times


Забележка : сезонните итеми ще бъдат извадени от дропа на не-сезона!


Нови легендарни итеми :

Iceblink (gem) [Seasonal]

Your Cold skills now apply Chill effects and your Chill effects now slow enemy movement speed by an additional 5%
The additional movement speed reduction is increased by .4% per rank
At rank 25 you gain 10% increased chance to critically strike Chilled or Frozen enemies

Mutilation Guard (gem) [Seasonal]
Gain 10% melee damage reduction
Melee damage reduction is increased by 1% per rank
At rank 25 while below 50% life, you may move through enemies unhindered

In-Geom (1h sword)

Blood Brother (2h sword)

Grants a 15-20% chance to block attacks
Blocked attacks inflict 30% less damage

Corrupted Ashbringer (2h sword)

Chance on kill to raise a skeleton to fight for you
Upon accumulating 5 skeletons, they explode for 1000% weapon damage and the sword transforms into Ashbringer for a short time
Attacking with Ashbringer burns your target for 4500-6000% weapon damage as Holy

Eun-Jang-Do (dagger)

Attacking enemies below 17-20% life freezes them for 3 seconds

Arcstone (ring)

Lightning pulses periodically between all wearers of this item dealing 1000-1500% weapon damage

Bottomless Potion of Amplification [Seasonal]

Increases healing from all sources by 20-25% for 5 seconds

Bottomless Potion of Rejuvenation

Gain 20-30% of your maximum resource when used below 50% Life

Convention of the Elements (ring)

Gain 150-200% increased damage to a single element for 4 seconds
This effect rotates through the elements available to your class in the following order: Arcane, Cold, Fire, Holy, Lightning, Physical, Poison
Note: Cold is not included for Crusaders

Drakon's Lesson (bracers)

When your Shield Bash hits 3 or less enemies its damage is increased by 150-200% and 25% of the Wrath cost is refunded

Omnislash (belt)

Slash attacks in all directions

Mortick's Brace (bracers)

Wrath of the Berserker gains the effect of every rune

Dread Iron (belt) [Seasonal]

Ground Stomp causes an Avalanche

Blessed of Haull (belt) [Seasonal]

Justice spawns a Blessed Hammer when it hits an enemy

Belt of Transcendence (belt)

Summon a Fetish Sycophant when you hit with a Mana spender

Crashing Rain (belt)

Haunted Girdle (belt)

Haunt releases 1 extra Spirit

Omryn's Chain (belt)

Drop Caltrops when using Vault

Little Man's Finger (ring) [Seasonal]

Gargantuan instead summons three smaller Gargantuans each more powerful than the original

Nilfur's Boast (boots)

Increases the damage of Meteor by 100%
Increases the damage of Meteor by 150-200% when it hits 3 or less enemies

Ranslor's Folly (bracers) [Seasonal]

Energy Twister Periodically pulls in enemies within 30 yards

Spirit Guards (bracers) [Seasonal]

Your Spirit generators reduce your damage taken by 30-40% for 3 seconds


Преработени и бъфнати стари легендарни :

Puzzle Ring

The Treasure Goblin helper spawned by this item will now pick up items in a larger radius

Note: The spell effect will only be shown on 5 enemies

Lightning smite weapon damage increased from 150-180% to 600-750%. The internal cooldown has been greatly reduced

St. Archew's Gage

The first time an elite pack damages you, gain an absorb shield for 120-150% of your maximum Life for 15 seconds

Wyrdward

Chance to stun increased from 13-17% to 25-35%

Homunculus

Time to summon a Zombie Dog to your side reduced from 4-6 seconds to 2 seconds

Tiklandian Visage

Now displays a buff icon indicating the duration of the Horrify effect

No longer rolls against skill proc coefficients
