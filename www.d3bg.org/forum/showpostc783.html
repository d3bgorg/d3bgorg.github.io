Oще един играч, стигнал 100-ен парагон с всички герои:

http://www.diablofans.com/topic/87428-a-long-journey-finally-ended-world-first-p500/

...

Here my prefered Route: Core of Arreat (backwards) -> Skycrown Battlements (backwards) -> KD2 -> Bridge of Korsikk -> Rakkis Crossing -> Tower of the Damned Lvl 1 -> Arreat Crater Lvl 2

The route sometimes changed a bit, but most of the time i used that one.

I played most of the time on MP0, sometimes MP1 or MP2 but i always went back to MP0 because I made more EXP there.

Here are my records in terms of EXP per hour:

Wizard 42mil / h (that was pre hellfire ring and pre monsterpower, only using the 31%gem)
WD 75mil / h (with Hellfire Ring and Leorics Seal and 31%gem)
Barb 90mil / h (Hellfire / Leorics / 31%gem)
DH 80mil / h (Hellfire / Leorics / 31%gem)
Monk 80mil / h (Hellfire / no Leorics / 31%gem)

The best Items I ever found : IK Belt sold for 300mil, Lacunis sold for 350mil, few Items worth around 100mil and many more worth around 1-50 mil.
Nothing special, no 1bil items... Maybe bad luck, maybe not.

...

how long did it take to level up 1 class? for example, monk, or barb?
Wizard ~ 450 hours (pre Hellfire Ring, pre MP that means inferno act 3 was like MP 2.5 but only rewarded the same EXP MP0 rewards nowadays)
WD ~ 240 hours
Barb ~190 hours
DH ~210 hours
Monk ~200 hours
