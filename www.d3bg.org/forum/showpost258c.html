Влияе и на ареа damage-a

ето и цитат от темата
Цитат:
The TotD is going to be the BiS mojo if you are using Horrify: Frightening Aspect (FA) (discussed in build). The pick-up radius bonus is insane and offers you the freedom to move about the map, while also triggering Grave Injustice. This will allow your spells to cooldown faster, which means things like Soul Harvest, Spirit Walk and Horrify will be available to cast more often. (Caveat-any mojo will do this, but TotD gives you the freedom to do so from a safe distance. It also will proc GI when enemies kill things, which might be out of GI’s innate range.) It also means you don’t have to walk excessively close to gold piles, which is always a plus. Double plus, but in situations where shiz is hitting the fan, you’re about to die, and there is a globe you otherwise wouldn’t be able to get to, you can inch around until you pick it up, from afar, and jump back into the fight.
